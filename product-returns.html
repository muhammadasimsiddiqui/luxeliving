Product Returns Policy

Introduction

At LuxeLiving, we strive to ensure your complete satisfaction with your purchase. If you're not entirely happy with your order, our returns policy is designed to make the return process as smooth and hassle-free as possible.

Return Period

You have 30 days from the date of delivery to initiate a return. We kindly ask that items are returned in their original condition, unused, and with all packaging and tags intact.

How to Initiate a Return

To return a product, please follow these steps:

  • Contact our customer service team at support@luxeliving.com to initiate your return.
  • Provide your order number and a brief description of the reason for the return.
  • Our team will guide you through the return process and provide you with a Return Authorization Number (RAN).
  • Carefully package the item in its original packaging, including all accessories, manuals, and documentation.
  • Write the RAN clearly on the outside of the package.
  • Ship the package to the address provided by our customer service team, and keep your shipping receipt for tracking.

Refund Process

Once we receive your returned item, we will inspect it and notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will be applied to your original payment method within 7-10 business days.

Return Exceptions

Certain items are not eligible for return, including:

  • Gift cards
  • Items marked as "final sale"
  • Personalized or customized products
  • Opened health and personal care products

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please reach out to us immediately at support@luxeliving.com for assistance.
