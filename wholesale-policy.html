Wholesale Policy for LuxeLiving

Introduction

Welcome to the LuxeLiving wholesale program! We are excited to partner with retailers and businesses who share our passion for quality and elegance. Our wholesale policy outlines the terms and conditions for purchasing our products at wholesale rates.

Eligibility

To qualify for our wholesale program, you must operate a registered retail business. We require valid documentation, including a business license and resale certificate, to verify your eligibility.

Minimum Order Requirements

Our wholesale orders have a minimum purchase requirement. The details are as follows:

  • Minimum initial order: $500
  • Reorders: Minimum of $300

Pricing and Discounts

Wholesale prices vary by product category. Discounts are available based on order volume:

  • Orders over $1,000: 10% discount
  • Orders over $5,000: 15% discount

Please contact our wholesale department for a full price list and additional discount structures.

Shipping and Handling

Shipping costs are calculated based on the weight and dimensions of the order. We ship domestically and internationally. Please allow 5-10 business days for processing and shipping. Tracking information will be provided upon shipment.

Returns and Exchanges

We want you to be completely satisfied with your wholesale purchase. If you encounter any issues, please refer to our return policy:

  • Returns accepted within 30 days of receipt.
  • Products must be unused and in original packaging.
  • Contact us to obtain a Return Authorization Number (RAN).

How to Apply

To join our wholesale program, please complete the following steps:

  • Fill out our wholesale application form available on our website.
  • Submit your business documentation via email to wholesale@luxeliving.com.
  • Upon approval, you will receive a welcome email with your wholesale pricing and terms.
